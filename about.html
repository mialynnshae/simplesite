A Few Words About Me

My name is Mialynn Dotson. I am a senior completing my Musical Theatre and Digital Marketing BFA degree. I grew up in Fort Collins, Colorado and have a large family, originating with 7 members, and now it continues to grow as my siblings get married and have kids! I am best known for...

Music

Singing, others and myself would consider to be my #1 hobby. I also learned to play the piano at a young age and play guitar, ukulele, and cello.

Books

Another thing I am incredibly interested in is reading and writing. I have always loved to read fantasy, adventure, and science fiction novels, and have been working on writing my own fantasy series for years now.

Travel

I more recently developed an interest in travel, but now it has become one of the biggest goals in my life. I have traveled outside the country and recently had an internship that took me all over the US. I plan to study abroad in Iceland in January and beyond graduation will take a gap year to travel Europe.
